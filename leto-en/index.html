Production

WINTER PRODUCTION
In winter we grow our products in closed ground in Uzbekistan and Russia. The gross area covers 150 hectares.

During summer, the hottest period in the UAE, we can also provide the customer with fresh leaf crops.

Many years of experience allow us to build optimal logistics for ultra-fresh products by outdoor temperatures. We also offer seasonal products made in Iran from November to March.

SUMMER PRODUCTION
An acreage of 300 hectares of open ground in Russia allows us to obtain a full range of organic spicy herbs and salads

from May to October and provide southern countries with fresh leaf crops during the hottest season.
